Site Purpose

The site provides essential beginner resources on sustainable living, including categorized lifestyle tips (food, home, travel), a carbon footprint calculator, and tools to track and improve personal eco habits.

Scenarios

  • What are the easiest changes I can make to live more sustainably at home?
  • How can I calculate and track my carbon footprint based on my daily habits?
