Choose local, seasonal produce to reduce your carbon footprint. Cut down on single-use plastics by using reusable containers.

Opt for public transportation or carpooling whenever possible. Consider walking or cycling for short trips.

Switch to energy-efficient appliances and LED bulbs. Conserve water by fixing leaks and using low-flow fixtures.

Reduce paper waste by going digital. Encourage green office initiatives like recycling programs and plant-based lunches.

Exercise outdoors when possible to save on gym energy use. Use eco-friendly yoga mats and reusable water bottles.
