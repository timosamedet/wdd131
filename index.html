Akwa Ibom, Nigeria

Akwa Ibom is a State in the South-South geopolitical zone of Nigeria. It borders Cross River State to the east, Rivers State and Abia State to the West and north-west, and the Atlantic Ocean to the south. Of the 36 states, Akwa Ibom is the 30th largest in area and fifteenth most populous, with an estimated population of nearly 5.5 million as of 2016.
